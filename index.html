🚬 No Smoking Policy
▼
🚨 CRITICAL ⚠️ LEGAL
😤 JUDY 👀
"Is that... SMOKE?"

This is a non-smoking building. Smoking is strictly prohibited:

  • ❌ Inside any unit
  • ❌ On porches or patios
  • ❌ Near windows or vents
  • ❌ Within 15 feet of entrances, exits, operable windows, or vents

Why this matters: Smoke drifts to neighboring properties. Our neighbor Judy has family members with serious lung illness. Cigarette smoke also damages walls and finishes, creating costs for everyone.

Legal basis: San Francisco Health Code Article 19F, Section 1009.22 and California Government Code Sections 7596-7598.

If you smoke: Please do so well away from the building (e.g., at Alamo Square Park or public curb), away from all windows and entrances.

Violation consequence: Smoking inside is a lease violation and grounds for eviction affecting the entire house.

📢 Before Blocking the Driveway
▼
💬
"blocking driveway 1pm-6pm, keys on fireplace"
  1. Announce it in the group chat (30+ minutes notice when possible)

    Example: "blocking the driveway 1pm-6pm, keys on the fireplace"

  2. Check for cars: Before blocking the driveway, see if there's a car in the garage or common room
    • If there IS a car: leave your keys in an accessible spot so they can get out
    • Take a photo of where you left the keys
    • Send the photo to the group chat
📸 If You Need to Move Someone's Car
▼
🚗 ➡️
Document EVERYTHING with photos
  • If someone is blocking you, message the group chat with a photo
  • If they don't respond quickly, you may move their car (if they left keys)
  • After moving: Put the keys back in the EXACT same spot you found them
  • Take a photo and send it to the chat—they need to know where their car is
