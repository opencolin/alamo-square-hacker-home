**GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR ALL PROMPTS:**

1. **Style Consistency:** Use "comic book style," "meme format," "illustration style," or "photo realistic" consistently
2. **Color Palettes:** 
   - Positive outcomes: bright, warm colors (greens, golds, warm whites)
   - Negative outcomes: dark, cool colors (reds, blues, blacks)
   - Neutral: vibrant comic colors, neon for cyberpunk
3. **Text Integration:** Always mention text overlay placement ("text on shirt," "speech bubble," "caption at top")
4. **Emotional Tone:** Clearly state tone (dark humor, wholesome, satirical, motivational, chaotic)
5. **Resolution:** Specify "high resolution," "comic book quality," or "meme-ready"
6. **Background Detail:** Be specific about settings (apartment, hallway, park, office, space, etc.)
