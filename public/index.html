💨 Keeping Our Space Fresh & Healthy
▼
❤️ Health First ⚠️ Legal
🌬️ ❌
Keep smoke outside the building

This is a smoke-free building — and we take this seriously because we care about everyone's health, especially our neighbor Judy's family. 💚

No smoking:

  • Inside any unit
  • On porches or patios
  • Near windows or vents
  • Within 15 feet of entrances, exits, operable windows, or vents

Why? Smoke drifts and affects neighbors. Judy's family members have serious lung illness and deserve a healthy home. Plus, smoke damages walls and finishes, costing everyone money.

If you smoke: No worries! Just step outside to Alamo Square Park or the public curb, away from windows and doors. 🚶‍♂️

This is backed by SF Health Code Article 19F & California Government Code Sections 7596-7598.
