## 🚫 CRITICAL RULES

### No Furniture Without Approval
**ABSOLUTELY NO FURNITURE should be brought into the home without approval from the landlord/property manager.** This is non-negotiable.

### No Smoking Policy
**This is a non-smoking building.** Smoking is strictly prohibited:
- ❌ Inside any unit
- ❌ On porches or patios
- ❌ Near windows or vents
- ❌ Within 15 feet of entrances, exits, operable windows, or vents

**Why this matters:** Smoke drifts to neighboring properties. Our neighbor Judy has family members with serious lung illness. Cigarette smoke also damages walls and finishes, creating costs for everyone.

**Legal basis:** San Francisco Health Code Article 19F, Section 1009.22 and California Government Code Sections 7596-7598.

**If you smoke:** Please do so well away from the building (e.g., at Alamo Square Park or public curb), away from all windows and entrances.

**Violation consequence:** Smoking inside is a lease violation and grounds for eviction affecting the entire house.

## 🔐 Door & Security Rules

### Smart Door Locks

**Default Setting:** Circle / Up position  
The front and back doors should generally be set to the circle/up position all the time for regular access.

**❌ DO NOT use the "lock" symbol** unless you've set up your fingerprints on that door knob. You will be locked out otherwise.

### Key Management
- **Start a habit:** Always put your keys in the same place every time
- **Have a backup:** Keep a spare key in a specific, known spot
- Building good habits prevents frustration and lockouts

## 🚗 Parking Rules

This home has tandem parking. We've developed these rules over 8 years of shared use.

### Before Blocking the Driveway
1. **Announce it in the group chat** (30+ minutes notice when possible)  
   Example: "blocking the driveway 1pm-6pm, keys on the fireplace"

2. **Check for cars:** Before blocking the driveway, see if there's a car in the garage or common room
   - If there IS a car: **leave your keys in an accessible spot** so they can get out
   - Take a photo of where you left the keys
   - Send the photo to the group chat

### If You Need to Move Someone's Car
- If someone is blocking you, message the group chat with a photo
- If they don't respond quickly, you may move their car (if they left keys)
- After moving: **Put the keys back in the EXACT same spot** you found them
- Take a photo and send it to the chat—they need to know where their car is

### If You're Blocked In/Out
- Message the group chat immediately with a photo
- If inconvenienced and need to leave: Take an Uber
- **The person blocking you will reimburse your round-trip Uber cost**
- Don't have spare keys? The person blocking you will move their car

### Street Parking & Legal Rules
- ✅ It IS legal to block our own driveway
- ✅ It IS legal to park in the driveway
- ❌ It is NOT legal to block the sidewalk (though tickets are rare)
- ⚠️ Street sweeping tickets suck: **Set a recurring phone reminder to move by 8am** on street sweeping days

### If You See an Unknown Car
- Message the group chat with a photo
- Let everyone know it's parked in the driveway/blocking the driveway

### Can't Help with Shuffling?
- If you're too busy to move cars, ask another housemate to help
- Communication is key
